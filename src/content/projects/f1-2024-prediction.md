With six races remaining in the 2024 season, I used Monte Carlo simulations, Excel macros, and a
Canva-built report to model championship odds for Verstappen, Norris, and Leclerc.

The write-up was later published in an Italian motorsport magazine — proof that a passion project,
done with real method, can travel.
